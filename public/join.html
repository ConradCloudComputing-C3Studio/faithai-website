What is the FaithAI Movement?

The FaithAI Movement is a Christ-centered initiative that harnesses the power of artificial intelligence to spread the Gospel and strengthen believers worldwide. We believe that technology, when surrendered to God's purposes, can be a powerful tool for advancing His Kingdom and drawing hearts to Jesus Christ.

Through our AI agents, we create daily devotional content, prayer support, and community engagement tools that are biblically sound, Gospel-centered, and designed to help believers grow deeper in their relationship with Jesus.
